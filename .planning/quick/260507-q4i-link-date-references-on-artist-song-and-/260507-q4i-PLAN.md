<design_decision>
StatsBar approach: extend StatsBar to accept an optional `href` per item (option (a) in constraints).

Justification: StatsBar is a tiny component (32 lines) and the change is fully backward-compatible — items without `href` continue to render as a plain `<p>`. A wrapper would duplicate the styling boilerplate (length-based font sizing, accent color) at every call site for very little gain. One small additive prop in one place is cleaner than three near-identical wrappers across song/album pages.
</design_decision>
